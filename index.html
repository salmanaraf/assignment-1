sustainability

ustainability means meeting our own needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. In addition to natural resources, we also need social and economic resources.

infrastructure

a proposed plan that focuses on the improvement and upkeep of services, facilities and systems already in place throughout a country.

empowerment

Empowerment is the degree of autonomy and self-determination in people and in communities. This enables them to represent their interests in a responsible and self-determined way, acting on their own authority.
